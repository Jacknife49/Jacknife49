## Hello, I'm an aspiring young Researcher and upcoming Graduate Student

Welcome to my GitHub profile. I am passionate about exploring the intersection of machine learning, sequential decision-making, and societal impact. My work primarily focuses on how algorithms interact with, adapt to, and influence the environments in which they are deployed.

---

### Research Interests

I am actively exploring and contributing to the following areas:

* **Performative Learning:** Understanding and modeling the effects of predictive models that alter the underlying data distribution they aim to predict.
* **Performative Reinforcement Learning (PRL):** Designing RL agents that account for environment dynamics shifting in response to the deployment of the agent's policy.
* **Multi-armed Bandit Algorithms:** Developing efficient exploration and exploitation strategies for sequential decision-making under uncertainty.
* **Algorithmic Fairness:** Ensuring algorithmic systems operate equitably and mitigate bias across diverse populations.
* **Differential Privacy:** Safeguarding sensitive information and establishing robust guarantees within machine learning pipelines.
* **Performative Privacy:** Investigating the complex interplay between privacy-preserving mechanisms and performative shifts in data generation.
